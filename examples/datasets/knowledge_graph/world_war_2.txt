The Liberation of Paris took place in August 1944, marking a turning point in World War II.
Paris is the capital of France and had been under German occupation.
London is the capital of the United Kingdom and endured the Blitz during the war.
The London Blitz took place from 1940 to 1941, with bombing raids targeting the city.
Rome is the capital of Italy and was liberated by Allied forces in June 1944.
The Battle of Monte Cassino took place near Rome in early 1944.
